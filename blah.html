...desire to make progress in life, (C 2024)

Everyone desires to make progress in life. While some make progress, there several people whose life take the retrogressive direction. The fact that there are poeple making progress in life means it is attainable. Making progress in life is not achieved by accident. it is a result of intentionality, taking deliberate steps and knowing what to do right. What separates the achiever from the failure is their exposure to knowledge and what they make of the knowledge acquired. This means that, it is not enough to know the secrets for making progress, it is essential to make every effort to put them to use by practicising them. A study into the lives of people who are making progress, below are few secrets they share as principles for making progress.
